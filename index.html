Asteroids are small, rocky objects that orbit the Sun. Although asteroids orbit the Sun like planets, they are much smaller than planets.

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. At about one-quarter the diameter of Earth, the largest satellite in the Solar System relative to its major planet.

A star is an astronomical object comprising a luminous spheroid of plasma held together by its gravity. The nearest star to Earth is the Sun.

A planet is a large astronomical body that is neither a star nor a stellar remnant. At least eight planets exist in the Solar System
